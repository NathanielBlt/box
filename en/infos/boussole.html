Feeling a bit lost?

Don’t worry, we’ve taken care of everything!

The University of Lille has five campuses that are sometimes like little towns, with classrooms, laboratories, librairies, sports complexes, cafés, concert venues, vegetable gardens… but lots of useful services, too.
You’ll find information, reception areas and staff to guide you at each university site.
The campuses are sometimes very big – at the start of the academic year, set aside time to find the student affairs office and your classrooms.
If you know the name of your faculty (see the “Contacts” tab of the course file), then you can find out where it is by clicking on the following links:
Headquarters of the University of Lille (siege de l’Université de Lille)

Cité Scientifique Campus

Faculties for sciences and technologies (FST) and economic and social sciences and territories (FSEST − anthropology, sociology, culture / economy, management / geography, planning), department of sciences of education and training of adults (SEFA), engineering schools Polytech’Lille and Institut Mines - Télécom, and University Institute of Technology A (IUT A − also on the Recueil site in Villeneuve d'Ascq)

Flers-Château Campus

National Institute for Higher Professorship and Education − Academy of Lille – Hauts-de-France (INSPÉ Lille HdF)

Moulins-Lille Campus

IAE Lille University School of Management (recently formed through the merger of IAE Lille and faculty for finances, banking, accountancy / Institute of marketing and management of distribution − FFBC/IMMD), faculty for legal, political and social sciences (FSJPS - also on the University Centre of Cambrai “La Forêt”).

Pont-de-Bois Campus

Faculties for the humanities (except plastic arts section), for languages, literature and overseas civilisations (LLCE − contacts), Deccid and for psychology.

When you know your room number, you can find it more quickly on this website.

Roubaix-Tourcoing Campus

Roubaix: faculty for finances, banking, accountancy / institute of marketing and management of distribution (FFBC/IMMD), University Institute of Technology C (IUT C), Deccid faculty − Infocom department, faculty for applied foreign languages (LEA)
Tourcoing: faculty for humanities − section plastic arts, University Institute of Technology B (IUT B)

Health Campus

Loos/Lille: Faculties for dental surgery, for medicine, pharmacy and engineering and health management (Ilis).
