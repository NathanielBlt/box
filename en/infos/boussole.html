Feeling a bit lost?

Don’t worry, we’ve taken care of everything!

The University of Lille has five campuses that are sometimes like little towns, with classrooms, laboratories, librairies, sports complexes, cafés, concert venues, vegetable gardens… but lots of useful services, too.
If you took part in the visits to the campus during the new student days in September (the “Jivé”), then that’s great. Otherwise, you’ll find information, reception areas and staff to guide you at each university site.
The campuses are sometimes very big – at the start of the academic year, set aside time to find the student affairs office and your classrooms.
If you know the name of your faculty (see the “Contacts” tab of the course file), then you can find out where it is by clicking on the following links:
Headquarters of the University of Lille (siege de l’Université de Lille)

Cité Scientifique Campus

Faculties for sciences and technologies (FST) and economic and social sciences (FSES), department of geography and urban planning, department of sciences of education and training of adults (SEFA), engineering schools Polytech’Lille and Institut Mines - Télécom, and University Institute of Technology A (IUT A − also on the Recueil site in Villeneuve d'Ascq)
Associated with this campus is the university management school (IAE) in Lille.

Moulins-Ronchin Campus

Faculties for finances, banking, accountancy / Institute of marketing and management of distribution (FFBC/IMMD), for legal, political and social sciences (FSJPS - also on the University Centre of Cambrai “La Forêt”) and sciences of sport and physical education (FSSEP).

Pont-de-Bois Campus

Faculties for the humanities (except plastic arts section), for languages, literature and overseas civilisations (LLCE − contacts), for historic, artistic and political sciences (Shap), faculty for social development, education, culture, communication, information, documentation (Deccid), for mathematics, information technology, management and economics (Mime) and for psychology.
When you know your room number, you can find it more quickly on this website.

Roubaix-Tourcoing Campus

Roubaix: faculty for finances, banking, accountancy / institute of marketing and management of distribution (FFBC/IMMD), University Institute of Technology C (IUT C), Deccid faculty − Infocom department, faculty for applied foreign languages (LEA)
Tourcoing: faculty for humanities − section plastic arts, University Institute of Technology B (IUT B)

Health Campus

Faculties for dental surgery, for medicine, pharmacy and engineering and health management (Ilis).
