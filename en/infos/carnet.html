Help, I’ve made a mistake!

You’ve just started your first year, but after the first few lessons, you can see it’s not going to work out… Then you need to act fast and contact the SUAIO. The staff will tell you what you can do, depending on your course and your project (possibility to change subjects at the end of the 1st term: start of the procedure in November)
But remember, reorientation is not automatic… We know it’s tempting just to pull the covers over your head, but don’t give up! If your reorientation is not accepted, you will need to start again from scratch the following year. So continue to work and you can get some credits, so you won’t have to take them again in your new course.
Between mid-January and mid-March, you can make a new choice on the Parcoursup platform
A tip: take part in the “SUAIO workshops” to help think about and develop a new educational project.

Where can you find them?

The SUAIO are located on three sites, and are on hand during new-student days (the “Jivé”), student fair or open days: don’t hesitate to come and meet them!
