Feeling peckish?

The canteen isn’t like it used to be

At university, people don’t say “canteen”, but “university restaurant” (or “RU”)! But the university is not in charge of the cooking – this is the job of the Crous (the same organisation that takes care of grants and university residences).
At the time of writing, a meal costs €3.25 for 1 dish and 3 “extras” (starter, cheese, desserts).
But to pay for a meal, you need to charge your card beforehand via an application for your smartphone or online (“Izly”). Some help here.
Apart from the university restaurants, there are often several places on campuses where you can buy a sandwich, pasta or salad.
On some campuses, there are areas where you can heat up your own meals in a microwave
There are sometimes also cafés and other areas run by associations (“Ulysse” on the Pont-de-Bois campus, MDE on the science campus, etc.)
Organic fruits and vegetables directly on campus : it's sometimes possible (contact information).

Off campus

With your student card, you can have meals in all the region’s universities (here is a map of the university restaurants and other restaurants).
When you have some spare time, don’t hesitate to go off campus. In Roubaix, head for Rue de l’Alma or near the train station. At Pont de Bois, there are fast-food restaurants on the other side of the footbridge. And in the centre of Villeneuve d’Ascq by metro (or on foot, when the weather’s fine), there’s a big shopping centre.

Your university mug

During the new student days (Jivé), mugs are given out – the aim is to avoid polluting the planet with plastic cups – and you also get 10 centimes off your drink! (in Crous restaurants)
