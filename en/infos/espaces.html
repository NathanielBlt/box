Working – a good idea, but where?

Digital, rooms and libraries – we’ll help you become more independent.

For work

In Villeneuve d’Ascq: There is Lilliad at the Cité scientifique. This is a “learning centre”, a vast with libraries, connected group-work areas, a cafeteria, etc.! At Pont-de-Bois, there is an university library (“BU SHS”) with a relaxation area, as well as other more specialised (“associated”) libraries. And if you feel like a change, there is also a municipal library.
In Roubaix, the Infocom library and the marketing & management/languages library, and in Tourcoing, the University Institute of Technology B library
In Lille, there are a law-management library, the business school library and Sciences Po library, which is open to university students until 10 p.m., Monday to Sunday. There are also ten municipal libraries in Lille, generally open from Tuesday to Saturday.
In Loos, there is a health library, open until 11 p.m., Monday to Friday.
There are also rooms equipped with computers and for working in groups.
To make progress in languages, head for the language resource centre (CRL in French): here you can read international magazines, watch videos, assess your level, get tips, make an appointment to talk with an overseas student, etc.

And what about on the internet?

In the ENT you can:
see exam results, the staff directory, university news and official documents;
take online lessons (the Moodle platform);
take care of administrative tasks: registration, transfer to another university, study abroad, internship conventions, etc.

To make sure you miss out on nothing, your university e-mail address

You will be given an e-mail address in the following format: firstname.lastname.etu@univ-lille.fr. It is very important (for exam dates, grants, etc.).
