Working – a good idea, but where?

Digital, rooms and libraries – we’ll help you become more independent.

For work

In Villeneuve d’Ascq: There is Lilliad at the Cité scientifique. This is a “learning centre”, a vast and brand-new area with libraries, group-work areas, a cafeteria, etc.! At Pont de Bois, there is a big university library (with a relaxation area), as well as other more specialised (“associated”) libraries open to all students. And if you feel like a change, there is also a municipal library ten minutes away on foot from the Pont de Bois campus.
In Roubaix, the Infocom library and the marketing & management/languages library, and in Tourcoing, the University Institute of Technology B library
In Lille, there are health and law-management libraries, the business school library and Sciences Po library, which is open to university students until 10 p.m., Monday to Sunday. There are also ten municipal libraries in Lille, generally open from Tuesday to Saturday.

Languages and I.T.

There are also rooms equipped with computers and for working in groups.
To make progress in languages, head for the language resource centre (CRL in French): here you can read international magazines, watch videos, assess your level, get tips, make an appointment to talk with an overseas student, etc.

And what about on the internet?

Need some help? All the information is here. You can also use a computer in the I.T. rooms. In the Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) you can:
see exam results, the staff directory, university news and official documents; read the press free of charge (Mediapart, etc.), plus specialised magazines.
take online lessons (the Moodle platform) and print out documents in the computer rooms.
take care of administrative tasks: registration, transfer to another university, study abroad, internship conventions, etc.

To make sure you miss out on nothing, your university e-mail address

You will be given an e-mail address in the following format: firstname.lastname.etu@univ-lille.fr. It is very important (for exam dates, grants, etc.). You can consult messages on the VLE or on your smartphone.

How can I get wifi access?

Just connect to the ”Aide Wifi” (Wifi Help) network and then follow the instructions.
